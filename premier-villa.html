About the villa

A private coastal retreat for families and groups

Premier Villa is a spacious four-bedroom private villa designed for families and groups seeking privacy, comfort and easy access to the coast.

The villa accommodates up to 8 adults and 4 children. Guests can enjoy a private swimming pool, bright bedrooms, balconies and peaceful tropical surroundings.

The beachside resort setting provides a relaxing atmosphere, landscaped gardens and convenient internal transportation. Online check-in is available before arrival.
