About the property

Modern serviced apartments for flexible stays

Luxury English House is an eight-unit serviced apartment building designed for short stays, extended stays, business travellers, couples and small families.

Guests can choose between two studios, four one-bedroom apartments and two two-bedroom apartments. Each unit is designed with modern interiors, practical storage, workspace and a comfortable private bathroom.

Shared facilities include a central swimming pool, elevator, laundry area and co-working space. Online check-in is available before arrival for a smoother and more convenient experience.
