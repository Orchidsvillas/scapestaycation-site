About the villa

A private stay created for comfort

Orchids Villas offers a modern one-bedroom villa experience with a private swimming pool and thoughtfully selected amenities. The villa is suited to couples, solo travellers and guests who value privacy, convenience and a comfortable place to unwind.

The bedroom includes a comfortable bed, lounge seating, television, storage, air conditioning and practical guest facilities. The kitchenette provides the essentials for light cooking and longer stays.

Guests can complete the online check-in process before arrival. After the booking and identification documents are verified, access instructions can be provided for a smooth self check-in.
